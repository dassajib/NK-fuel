Customization

Nowadays, customers require a customized fleet for fuel transport. Considering the customer demand, we bring changes in our fleet and add more features to meet their requirements.

Container Thickness

We provide a 4-layered compartment facility inside the vehicle. Even if any vehicle gets into an accident, there is no way for the fuel to outflow from the tank due to this feature. Moreover, the wall of the tank is made of 7.5 mm plate to make it even more durable. Thus overall, it ensures fuel safety inside the container.

Rubber Base

We have most modern vehicles from Ashok Leyland for the transportation of the Fuel. Our tankers are placed on Rubber base on the chasis of the vehicle to ensure longevity of the vehicle. Thus all our vehicles are well-equipped with possible safety systems.

Compliance

We maintain 100% compliance with the local transportation system, for example, updated driving licenses, vehicle registration, tax token, vehicle fitness, route permit etc. for providing uninterrupted services to our customers. All the 45 fleets of N.K Fuel Transport Limited are certified by the BSTI.
