DIGITAL TRACKING SYSTEM


N.K Fuel Transport Limited always believes in the safety of your fuel. Our Innovative Transport Management System enables us to work even more efficiently. It is one of the innovative developments of N.K Fuel with which we have established a real-time connection with our clients. As all of our vehicles are controlled by a digital tracking system, clients can track real-time location of the fleet by using a smart phone or computer. We can share the order status, the loading and unloading times, the truck location, and even the stock level through this digital tracking system. Anyone can track their transports live and be well-informed of the latest state of affairs.
