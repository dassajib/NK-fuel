ABOUT US

N.K Fuel Transport Limited is a fuel transportation and logistics solutions company which started its journey in Bangladesh in 2017. Within this small period of time it has proved itself to be a leader in the industry by introducing state of the art technologies and innovation in fuel transportation system.

Our Mission

To provide innovative distribution solutions, driven by service, where our customer satisfaction is the critical parameter.

Our Vision

To be the market leader in specialized logistics and fuel transportation service.






OUR GOAL

Our goal is to become the country's leading logistics solutions provider in fuel oil transportation and to expand our reputation across Bangladesh and beyond. To achieve this goal, we consistently try to keep ourselves a step ahead of the other players in the market by availing the best people, technology, ideas, values, and equipment. We also make it our goal to reduce all safety and quality lapses to zero, in doing so, improving the work atmosphere for our team members, and creating added value for our clients.
